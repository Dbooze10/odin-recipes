Garlic Penne Pasta

Calling all my pasta LOVERS! This is a delicious, super quick side or main dish. Very creamy with a nice touch of garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb box penne
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 3/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons parsley flakes, salt and pepper
  • 1/3 cup grted parmesan cheese
